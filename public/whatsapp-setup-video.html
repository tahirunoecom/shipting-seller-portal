🎥 WhatsApp Business API Setup Guide

Follow these steps to connect your WhatsApp Business account

Step 1 of 10

Login with Facebook

Navigate to the WhatsApp integration page on your seller portal and locate the blue "Login with Facebook" button. Click this button to initiate the WhatsApp Business API setup process. You'll need an existing Facebook account with Business Manager access to proceed. Make sure you're logged into the correct Facebook account before clicking.

Step 2 of 10

Continue as Your Profile

A popup window will appear asking you to "Continue as [Your Name]". This dialog shows what permissions the application will request, including the ability to communicate with customers at scale and send messages with optimizations. Review the terms and privacy policy, then click the blue "Continue" button to proceed. This confirms you have permission to set up the business account and grants the necessary access for WhatsApp integration.

Step 3 of 10

Select Business Assets

You'll now see a page titled "Select the business assets to share". Here you need to configure three key components: Business Portfolio (your Meta Business Manager portfolio), WhatsApp Business Account (your WhatsApp Business account), and Catalogue (your product catalog for showcasing items). You can either select existing assets from the dropdown menus or create new ones if this is your first time setting up. Make your selections carefully as these will be linked to your seller portal.

Step 4 of 10

Enter Business Information - CRITICAL STEP!

This is a crucial step! Fill in your business details accurately: Name (your business name), Category (select the type that best describes your business like Restaurant, Retail, etc.), Country (your business location), Website (your business URL if available), and Time Zone (your local time zone). ⚠️ CRITICAL: In the "Vertical" dropdown, you MUST select "Commerce". This is absolutely required for product catalog features to work correctly. Don't skip this!

Step 5 of 10

Add Your WhatsApp Phone Number

Now you need to add your WhatsApp Business phone number. You have three options: "Use a display name only" (limited features), "Add a new number" (recommended - use your business phone), or "Use existing number" (if already registered). Select "Add a new number", choose your country code from the dropdown (e.g., US +1), and enter your phone number. Then choose how you'd like to receive the verification code: "Text message" (SMS) or "Phone call". We recommend Text message for faster verification. Click "Next" when ready.

Troubleshooting - Common Issue

Phone Number Already Exists Error

If you encounter an error message stating "To use this phone number, you'll need to delete an existing one from WhatsApp Manager or request additional numbers on your account", this means the phone number you entered is already registered with another WhatsApp Business account. You have three options to resolve this: 1) Go to WhatsApp Manager and delete the existing registration, 2) Use a different phone number, or 3) Request additional number capacity from Meta. Click "Report error to AnythingInstantly!" if you believe this is a mistake.

Alternative - Use Existing Number

Use Existing WhatsApp Number

If you already have a WhatsApp Business number registered in your Meta Business account, you'll see an option to "Use an existing number". Select this option and choose your number from the dropdown list. This is useful if you've previously set up WhatsApp Business and want to connect the same number to this seller portal. The existing number will already be verified, so you can skip the verification step.

Step 6 of 10

Review and Confirm Permissions

You'll see a final review page titled "Review what you'll share with AnythingInstantly". This page shows all the permissions and access you're granting, including access to your Business (business portfolio), WhatsApp Business account (to manage messages and conversations), and Catalogs (to manage product listings). The app will be able to: manage your product catalogues, manage your business, manage your WhatsApp accounts, and manage conversations in WhatsApp. Review the permissions carefully, and if everything looks correct, click the blue "Confirm" button to finalize the integration.

Step 7 of 10 - Success!

✓ Account Successfully Connected

Congratulations! You've successfully connected your WhatsApp Business account to the seller portal. You'll see a confirmation message: "Your account is connected to AnythingInstantly". Important: Meta will now review your business to ensure it complies with WhatsApp's Commerce Policy. This review typically takes up to 24 hours. You'll receive a notification once approved. You can optionally add a payment method now by clicking "Add payment method", or click "Finish" to complete the setup. You can always add payment details later in Business settings.

Step 8-10 of 10 - Next Steps

Sync Product Catalog & Start Selling

After your account is approved, the final steps are to sync your product catalog with WhatsApp and configure your messaging templates. Your product catalog will automatically sync from your seller portal, allowing customers to browse and purchase products directly through WhatsApp conversations. You can also set up automated order notifications, shipping updates, and customer support messages. Visit the "Messages" and "Catalog" tabs on this page to configure these features. Your WhatsApp Business integration is now complete and ready to use!
