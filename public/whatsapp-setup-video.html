🎥 WhatsApp Business API Setup Guide

Follow these steps to connect your WhatsApp Business account

Step 1 of 9

Login with Facebook

Click "Login with Facebook" button to start the WhatsApp Business API setup process. You'll need an existing Facebook account to proceed.

Step 2 of 9

Continue as Your Profile

Facebook will ask you to confirm your identity. Click "Continue" to proceed with your Facebook profile. This confirms you have permission to set up the business account.

Step 3 of 9

Select Business Assets

Choose your Business Portfolio, WhatsApp Business account, and Product Catalog. If you don't have these set up yet, you can create new ones during this process.

Step 4 of 9

Enter Business Information

Fill in your business details including name, category, country, website, and time zone. Important: Make sure to select "Commerce" as your vertical for e-commerce features.

Step 5 of 9

Add Your WhatsApp Phone Number

Enter your business phone number. You can choose between "Add a new number" or "Use a display name only". For verification, select either Text message or Phone call.

Step 5 - Common Issue

Number Already Exists Error

If you see this error, it means the phone number is already registered. You'll need to delete it from WhatsApp Manager or use a different number.

Step 5 - Alternative

Use Existing WhatsApp Number

If you already have a WhatsApp Business number registered, you can select it from the dropdown instead of adding a new one.

Step 6 of 9

Review Permissions

Review what access your application will have to your Business, WhatsApp Business account, and Catalogs. Click "Confirm" if you agree with the permissions.

Step 7 of 9

Success! Account Connected

Congratulations! Your WhatsApp Business account is now connected. Meta will review your business to ensure compliance with WhatsApp's Commerce Policy. You'll hear back within 24 hours.

Step 9 of 9

Sync Your Product Catalog

The final step is to sync your product catalog with WhatsApp. This allows customers to browse and purchase your products directly through WhatsApp.
