WhatsApp Setup Guide Screenshots
==================================

Place the following screenshot images in this folder.
These are shown in the WhatsApp Bot setup guide to help sellers
understand each step of the Meta Embedded Signup process.

Required files:
---------------
1. step1-login-facebook.png     - Our website showing "Login with Facebook" button
2. step2-continue-dialog.png    - Meta popup "Seamlessly connect your account to AnythingInstantly"
3. step3-business-assets.png    - Meta page showing Business portfolio, WhatsApp Account, Catalogue dropdowns
4. step4-business-info.png      - Meta page showing business name, category, country, website, timezone, Vertical="Commerce"
5. step5-add-number.png         - Meta page showing "Add a new number" selected with phone number field
6. step5-number-error.png       - Meta page showing error "To use this phone number, you'll need to delete..."
7. step5-existing-number.png    - Meta page showing "Use a new or existing WhatsApp number" with dropdown
8. step6-review-permissions.png - Meta page "Review what you'll share with AnythingInstantly"
9. step7-success.png            - Meta page "Your account is connected to AnythingInstantly" with Finish button
10. step9-sync-catalog.png      - Our website showing Product Catalog section with "Sync Products to Catalog" button

Image format: PNG recommended (JPG also works)
Recommended size: 600-1200px wide
