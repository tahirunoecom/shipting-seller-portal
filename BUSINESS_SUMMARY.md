# Shipting Seller Portal - Complete Business Overview

## What Is This Platform?

Shipting is a complete **e-commerce order fulfillment platform** that helps store owners sell products through WhatsApp, manage orders, and deliver them to customers - either by themselves or through local delivery partners.

Think of it as your complete online store management system where everything happens in one place: from receiving orders on WhatsApp to getting paid in your bank account.

---

## Who Can Use This Platform?

The platform supports three types of users:

### 1. **Store Owners (Sellers)**
People who want to sell products and manage their online store

### 2. **Delivery Partners (Drivers)**
People who want to earn money by delivering orders for store owners

### 3. **Platform Administrators**
The team that manages the platform, approves sellers, and oversees operations

**Special Feature:** A person can be BOTH a seller and a driver at the same time! They can switch between roles anytime.

## Common Questions Answered

**Q: Do I need technical skills to use this?**
A: No! Everything is designed to be simple. If you can use WhatsApp and Facebook, you can use this platform.

**Q: How long does Stripe onboarding take?**
A: Usually 10-15 minutes for initial setup. Stripe may take 1-2 days to verify your account.

**Q: Can I use my existing WhatsApp Business number?**
A: Yes! You can connect your existing number. Or buy a new one through the platform.

**Q: How do drivers find my orders?**
A: When you choose "Find Driver", all drivers within a few miles get a notification. First one to accept gets the delivery.

**Q: When do I get paid?**
A: You request payouts whenever you want (minimum $50). Admin approves, and Stripe transfers to your bank in 2-7 business days.

**Q: What if I want to deliver some orders myself and use drivers for others?**
A: Perfect! Choose on an order-by-order basis. Each order, you decide: self or find driver.

**Q: Can I sell to customers outside WhatsApp?**
A: The platform is optimized for WhatsApp selling, but you can manually enter orders too.

**Q: What if a customer doesn't have WhatsApp?**
A: They can still place orders by calling/texting you, and you enter them manually.
