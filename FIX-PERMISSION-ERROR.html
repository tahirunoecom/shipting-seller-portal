🔧 Fix WhatsApp Catalog Permission Error

Error #200: You do not have permission to access this field

❌ What's Wrong?

Your access token is missing the catalog_management permission.

Even though the permission was approved on Dec 25, 2025, your current access token was generated WITHOUT it.

1

Go to WhatsApp Settings

Navigate to your Seller Portal:

  • Click on "WhatsApp" in the main menu
  • Or go to: /whatsapp page
2

Find the "Disconnect" Button

Look for a red button that says:

🔗 Disconnect
⚠️ Note: This button only appears if WhatsApp is currently connected. If you're already disconnected, skip to Step 3.
3

Click "Disconnect WhatsApp"

When you click the Disconnect button:

  • You'll see a confirmation dialog
  • Click "OK" or "Yes" to confirm
  • WhatsApp will be disconnected
💡 Don't worry! This is temporary. We're going to reconnect immediately with a fresh token.
4

Click "Connect with Facebook"

After disconnecting, you'll see a button like:

🔗 Connect with Facebook

Click this button to start the Facebook login flow again.

5

Complete Facebook Login

Follow the Meta Embedded Signup flow:

  • Login to Facebook (if needed)
  • Select your business account
  • Select your WhatsApp Business Account
  • Review permissions (catalog_management should be there now!)
  • Click "Continue" or "Confirm"
🔑 KEY POINT: The new access token will now include catalog_management permission because it was approved on Dec 25, 2025!
6

Test Again!

After reconnecting:

  • Open test-catalog-connection.html again
  • The access token will be automatically updated
  • Click "Connect Catalog to WABA"
  • You should see: ✅ SUCCESS!

✅ Expected Result

After reconnecting:

  • ✅ New access token with catalog_management permission
  • ✅ Catalog connects successfully
  • ✅ Error 131009 / Error 200 is GONE
  • ✅ Can send WhatsApp product messages!

🤔 Why Does This Work?

When you reconnect, Facebook generates a brand new access token based on the current approved permissions. Since catalog_management was approved on Dec 25, 2025, the new token will include it!

📞 Need Help?

If you still have issues after reconnecting:

  • Check that catalog_management shows in your app permissions
  • Wait 5 minutes after reconnecting before testing
  • Clear browser cache and try again
  • Check SOLUTION_SUMMARY.md for more troubleshooting
