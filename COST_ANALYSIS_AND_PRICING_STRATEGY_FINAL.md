## 🎯 RECOMMENDED PRICING MODEL: HYBRID

### Why Hybrid (Subscription + Commission)?

**Industry Research (2026):**
- 61% of successful SaaS platforms use hybrid models
- Provides predictable revenue (subscriptions) + growth-aligned revenue (commissions)
- Seller-friendly: Low risk for new sellers, clear ROI for growing sellers
- Best profit margins at scale: 80-92%

**Competitive Benchmark:**
- **Shopify**: $29-299/month + 2.9% transaction fee
- **Amazon Seller**: $39.99/month + 8-15% referral fee
- **Etsy**: $0.20 listing + 6.5% transaction + 3% payment processing
- **Stripe**: 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction

## 🎯 FINAL RECOMMENDATION: HYBRID MODEL

### Pricing Strategy

**FREE TIER:**
- Purpose: Attract sellers, prove value
- Limit: 50 orders/month (prevents abuse)
- Conversion trigger: Auto-upgrade prompt at 50 orders
- Target: Get 100 free users, convert 50% = 50 paid users

**GROWTH TIER ($12/month):**
- Sweet spot for most sellers
- Only $0.40/day = cost of 1-2 orders
- Unlimited orders removes growth concerns
- 3% commission is competitive with market

**PRO TIER ($30/month):**
- Premium features justify higher price
- 2% commission = lower fee for high volume
- API access enables custom integrations
- Daily payouts improve cash flow

**ENTERPRISE TIER (Custom):**
- Negotiated pricing for large accounts
- 1.5% commission = lowest fee structure
- Custom SLA and white-label options
- Dedicated support justifies premium pricing
