💵 Recommended Pricing Model: HYBRID

Why Hybrid (Subscription + Commission)?

  • Industry Standard: 61% of successful SaaS platforms use this model in 2026
  • Best of Both Worlds: Predictable revenue (subscriptions) + growth-aligned revenue (commissions)
  • Seller-Friendly: Low risk for new sellers (FREE tier), clear ROI for growing sellers
  • Competitive: Aligned with Shopify ($29-299/mo + 2.9%), Amazon ($40/mo + 8-15%), Etsy (6.5% + 3%)
  • Best Margins: 84-93% profit margins at scale
