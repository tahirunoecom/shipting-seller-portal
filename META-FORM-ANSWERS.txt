WHATSAPP BUSINESS SOLUTION PROVIDER FOR E-COMMERCE SELLERS

We operate a SaaS platform that enables small and medium-sized e-commerce businesses to integrate their product catalogs and business operations with WhatsApp Business Platform without requiring technical expertise or direct API development.

Our application uses the whatsapp_business_management permission to act as a Tech Provider / Solution Partner, managing WhatsApp Business Accounts on behalf of our clients through a unified dashboard.

================================================================================
SPECIFIC FEATURES AND API ENDPOINTS USED:
================================================================================

1. WHATSAPP BUSINESS ACCOUNT (WABA) MANAGEMENT

   API Endpoints Used:
   - GET /{whatsapp-business-account-id}
   - GET /{whatsapp-business-account-id}/phone_numbers
   - GET /{whatsapp-business-account-id}/message_templates

   What We Do:
   - Retrieve and display WhatsApp Business Account details in our dashboard
   - Show connected phone numbers and their status
   - Monitor account health metrics and quality ratings
   - Display messaging tier limits (Standard, Tier 1, Tier 2, Unlimited)
   - Track account verification status

   User Value:
   Sellers can view their entire WhatsApp Business setup in one place without logging into Meta Business Manager separately.

---

2. BUSINESS PROFILE MANAGEMENT

   API Endpoints Used:
   - GET /{phone-number-id}/whatsapp_business_profile
   - POST /{phone-number-id}/whatsapp_business_profile

   Profile Fields We Manage:
   - about (business description)
   - address (physical address)
   - description (detailed business info)
   - email (contact email)
   - websites (business website URLs)
   - vertical (business category/industry)
   - profile_picture_handle (profile photo)

   What We Do:
   - Allow sellers to view their current WhatsApp Business Profile
   - Enable one-click profile updates through our interface
   - Sync business information from their e-commerce store to WhatsApp
   - Update profile photos and cover images
   - Manage business hours and contact information

   User Value:
   Sellers maintain consistent branding across all channels without technical knowledge.

---

3. PHONE NUMBER REGISTRATION & VERIFICATION

   API Endpoints Used:
   - POST /{phone-number-id}/request_code
   - POST /{phone-number-id}/verify_code
   - POST /{phone-number-id}/register
   - GET /{phone-number-id}

   What We Do:
   - Guide sellers through phone number registration process
   - Request OTP verification codes via SMS or voice call
   - Submit verification codes on behalf of sellers
   - Complete phone number registration with WhatsApp
   - Monitor phone number quality ratings and status
   - Handle display name updates (subject to Meta review)

   User Value:
   100% self-service phone number setup without technical support or Meta representative involvement.

---

4. PRODUCT CATALOG MANAGEMENT (Primary Use Case)

   API Endpoints Used:
   - POST /{business-id}/owned_product_catalogs (create catalog)
   - GET /{catalog-id}
   - POST /{catalog-id}/products (add products)
   - POST /{catalog-id}/batch (bulk product operations)
   - DELETE /{catalog-id}/products/{product-id}
   - POST /{phone-number-id}/whatsapp_commerce_settings (link catalog)

   What We Do:
   - Automatically create WhatsApp Business Catalogs for sellers
   - Sync entire product inventory from e-commerce stores (Shopify, WooCommerce, custom)
   - Upload product details: names, descriptions, prices, images, SKUs, availability
   - Handle bulk product updates (add/update/delete hundreds of products)
   - Link catalogs to phone numbers for customer browsing
   - Manage product variants and categories
   - Update product availability in real-time

   Product Data Synced:
   - Product name and description
   - Price and currency
   - Product images (up to 10 per product)
   - SKU / product code
   - Availability status (in stock / out of stock)
   - Product URL (link to store)
   - Category and subcategory

   User Value:
   One-click synchronization of 100-1000+ products to WhatsApp Catalog. Sellers can showcase their entire inventory to WhatsApp customers instantly, without manual product entry.

---

5. MESSAGE TEMPLATE MANAGEMENT

   API Endpoints Used:
   - GET /{whatsapp-business-account-id}/message_templates
   - POST /{whatsapp-business-account-id}/message_templates
   - DELETE /{whatsapp-business-account-id}/message_templates/{template-name}

   What We Do:
   - Create message templates for customer communications
   - Submit templates to Meta for approval
   - Monitor template approval status
   - Manage template categories (MARKETING, UTILITY, AUTHENTICATION)
   - Handle template variables and placeholders
   - Delete rejected or outdated templates

   User Value:
   Pre-built message templates for common scenarios (order confirmations, shipping updates, promotional messages) with built-in WhatsApp Business Policy compliance.

---

6. COMMERCE SETTINGS MANAGEMENT

   API Endpoints Used:
   - GET /{phone-number-id}/whatsapp_commerce_settings
   - POST /{phone-number-id}/whatsapp_commerce_settings

   What We Do:
   - Link product catalogs to phone numbers
   - Enable shopping cart functionality
   - Configure commerce features (is_catalog_visible, is_cart_enabled)
   - Manage product visibility settings

   User Value:
   Customers can browse products, add to cart, and inquire about purchases directly in WhatsApp.

---

7. WEBHOOK SUBSCRIPTIONS (For Real-Time Features)

   API Endpoints Used:
   - POST /{whatsapp-business-account-id}/subscribed_apps
   - GET /{whatsapp-business-account-id}/subscribed_apps

   What We Do:
   - Subscribe to webhook events (messages, message_template_status_update, etc.)
   - Receive real-time notifications about incoming messages
   - Track message delivery status
   - Monitor template approval status changes

   User Value:
   Real-time message notifications and automated responses (chatbot features).

---

8. ANALYTICS AND INSIGHTS

   API Endpoints Used:
   - GET /{whatsapp-business-account-id}/conversation_analytics
   - GET /{phone-number-id} (includes quality_rating, messaging_limit_tier)

   What We Do:
   - Request conversation analytics data
   - Display messaging metrics in dashboard
   - Monitor quality ratings (Green/Yellow/Red)
   - Track messaging tier progression
   - Show conversation counts and costs

   User Value:
   Data-driven insights to optimize WhatsApp Business performance.

================================================================================
USER WORKFLOW (END-TO-END):
================================================================================

Step 1: SELLER REGISTRATION
- Seller signs up on our platform (e-commerce business owner)
- Completes basic business information

Step 2: OAUTH AUTHORIZATION (Shown in Video)
- Seller navigates to WhatsApp section in our dashboard
- Clicks "Connect WhatsApp" button
- Our app initiates Meta Embedded Signup (OAuth 2.0 flow)
- Seller redirected to Facebook for authentication
- Facebook displays permission request dialog
- Dialog shows: whatsapp_business_management, whatsapp_business_messaging, catalog_management, business_management
- Seller selects their WhatsApp Business Account
- Seller clicks "Continue" to grant permissions
- Facebook redirects back to our platform with authorization code

Step 3: TOKEN EXCHANGE (Backend)
- Our backend exchanges authorization code for access token
- Access token encrypted with AES-256 and stored securely
- Token refresh handled automatically

Step 4: PHONE NUMBER SETUP
- If needed, seller registers WhatsApp Business phone number
- We use whatsapp_business_management to request/verify OTP
- Phone number verified and activated

Step 5: PROFILE SETUP
- Seller updates business profile through our interface
- We call whatsapp_business_profile API to sync changes
- Profile visible to WhatsApp customers

Step 6: CATALOG SYNC (Primary Feature)
- Seller clicks "Sync Products to WhatsApp Catalog"
- Our system:
  - Creates WhatsApp Business Catalog (if doesn't exist)
  - Fetches products from seller's e-commerce store
  - Uploads products to catalog via batch API
  - Links catalog to phone number
- Confirmation: "Successfully synced X products to WhatsApp Catalog"

Step 7: ONGOING MANAGEMENT
- Seller manages everything through our dashboard:
  - Update business profile
  - Sync new products or update existing ones
  - Monitor messaging metrics
  - View customer inquiries
  - Send template messages

Step 8: CUSTOMER EXPERIENCE
- WhatsApp customers chat with seller's business number
- Click "View Catalog" in WhatsApp
- Browse products synced via our platform
- Add products to cart and inquire
- Complete purchases through WhatsApp conversation
